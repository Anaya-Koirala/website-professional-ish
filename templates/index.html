Hello, and Welcome ...


My name is Anaya Koirala, and I am currently pursuing Computer Science and Philosophy at Gettysburg College.↗

I aim to pursue research in computer science but still hope to engage in and incorporate more philosophy. I love learning and exploring, and as a result, I have wide-ranging interests in Computational Maths, Algorithms, Systems Programming, Programming Languages, Functional Programming (mostly OCaml and small traces of Haskell), Free and Open Source Software, Artificial Intelligence, UNIX-based Operating Systems, ...

Honestly, as I explore and learn, my interests evolve. Some fade, and most return in new and interesting ways. What is constant is my love of learning. However, I know that sounds kinda boring so here are a few things we can chat about: Continental Philosophy, Zhuangzi, Ethics, Postmodern Theory, Cormac McCarthy, World History, Jazz, Classical and Hindustani Raga music, ...

Anyways, there's always more to learn, explore, and engage with. I don't consider the list above definitive, so feel free to contact me to start a discussion, suggest a movie, or explain Monads to me.

~Anaya
