CS 111/112 — CS I & II

My fourth hour project for this class was an implementation of Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe in Flutter.

Rules

  • Played on a 3×3 grid of 3×3 tic-tac-toe boards.
  • The position of your mark determines which board the next player must play on.
  • If sent to a full or won board, you may play on any board.
  • Win three smaller boards in a row to win the game.
